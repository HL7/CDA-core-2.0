1 A: AdministrativeLocationRoleType V19598

A role type that is used to further qualify an entity playing a role where the role class attribute is set to RoleClassAdministrativeLocation.The role of a place that further classifies the setting in which administrative services are performed and/or to which a person may be assigned in a functional role.
